Shipping costs depend on product weight, destination and speed of delivery. Italy: Standard Shipping: 2-3 working days; Priority Shipping: untill 12:00 am of the day after the shipping. FAQ



How decks are made?


Decks are made in solid ash wood, selected because of the optimal ratio between flexibility – strength that permits the best performance of the product. The griptape is made with quartz sand and it’s totally clear, letting visible the upside graphic of the skateboard. Also the griptape is handmade, it ensures adherence to the deck distinguishes itself from the normal black one. If your purchase is a complete skateboard, please check trucks and screws, adjustable to your pleasure. Atypical deals to the whole process of creating the decks, all the other components are purchased from dealers and then assembled, trucks used are Slant and Speed Demons Abec 3 bearings. Here the complete production process.
